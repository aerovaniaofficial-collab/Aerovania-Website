Insurance Lines of Business

Expertise Across Every Line. Focus Where It Matters Most.

Nearly 80% of Fulcrum’s insurance work spans Property & Casualty, supported by active engagements in Life, Health, Pet, Travel, and Specialty lines.

From monoline commercial policies to complex multi-line programs, we understand the operational, regulatory, and data nuances that define each segment and engineer systems built to perform in real-world underwriting and claims environments.

  • Property & Casualty (Personal, Commercial, Excess & Surplus, Professional Liability)
  • Life & Annuities (Individual, Group, Term, Investment-Linked)
  • Accident & Health, Travel, Pet, and Specialty Insurance
  • Reinsurance operations and financial reporting
  • Regulatory alignment with NAIC, state, and global frameworks

Core Platform & System Modernization

Modern Insurance Cores, Built for Scale and Stability

Fulcrum modernizes policy, billing, and claims platforms into modular, API-first environments that unify customer, agent, and broker experiences. Modernization is accelerated through AI-assisted development tools that streamline configuration, testing, and release cycles.

Our engineers bring cross-platform expertise in Duck Creek, Guidewire, and Origami, enabling faster releases, lower cost of ownership, and seamless integration across digital channels.

  • Core re-platforming and modular API orchestration.
  • Low-code modernization with Fulcrum ONE (F1) and BYOP
  • Cloud enablement with AWS Textract and Azure DevOps
  • Continuous monitoring and automation for uptime assurance
  • AI-assisted SDLC acceleration for faster configuration and releases

Underwriting & Risk Automation

Accelerate Underwriting with Automation and Insight

We streamline underwriting from intake to binding through RPA, OCR, and AI-driven decision support.

Fulcrum’s automation initiatives—validated across leading carriers—reduce manual effort, shorten turnaround time, and enhance accuracy in document processing, triage, and risk evaluation.

  • Auto-indexing for endorsements, renewals, and premium finance documents
  • Predictive pricing and risk scoring using Tableau and Python models
  • Automated document workflows via ImageRight + AWS Textract
  • Underwriting Workbench integration for faster decisions
  • Efficiency gains up to 80–95 % in live implementations

Claims Modernization & Operations Headline:

Smarter Claims, Faster Resolutions

Fulcrum’s claims automation suite enables 24×7 processing with built-in scalability and auditability.

Through intelligent RPA, AI, and workflow orchestration, we help carriers accelerate FNOL, adjudication, and settlement, achieving measurable reductions in cycle time and cost while improving transparency for policyholders.

  • FNOL automation and digital self-service tracking portals
  • Auto-indexing for general claims and mail indexing
  • Adjuster assignment and TPA funding automation
  • Fraud analytics and predictive severity scoring
  • Integrations with ImageRight, Claims Assure, Mitchells IBM Notes

Financial & Back-Office Transformation

Automate Financial Operations with Audit-Ready Precision

Fulcrum digitizes insurance finance workflows through OCR, RPA, and intelligent document processing, cutting reconciliation time from days to hours while ensuring complete audit readiness.

Our automations strengthen financial accuracy, compliance visibility, and treasury management for insurers worldwide.

  • PDF data extraction and OCR-based reconciliation
  • RPA for collateral identification, refunds, and ledger updates
  • Integration with SAP, Oracle, PeopleSoft, Salesforce, and Coupa
  • Expense monitoring, profitability, and risk dashboards
  • 99% reconciliation accuracy and 40× faster processing

Governance, Compliance & Digital Resilience

Insurance Systems That Inspire Trust

Fulcrum embeds governance, model validation, and explainable-AI controls into every layer of the insurance technology stack.

We align with evolving frameworks such as NAIC, GDPR, and DORA to ensure every process is traceable, every decision auditable, and every platform secure, building digital resilience that lasts. Our data governance capabilities are strengthened by engineering partnerships such as Databricks, streamlining data lineage, quality controls, and regulatory reporting.

  • Automated regulatory monitoring (SOC 2, ISO 27001, NAIC)
  • AI governance and explainability frameworks
  • Policy orchestration and risk scoring automation
  • Privacy-by-design for customer and broker portals
  • Continuous compliance and resilience testing
