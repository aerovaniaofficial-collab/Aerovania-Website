About Aerovania

We build deep-tech AI and hardware platforms that give critical infrastructure a mind of its own — monitoring, learning, and evolving in real time.

Our Story

Engineering Tomorrow's Infrastructure

Aerovania was founded with a single conviction: India's critical infrastructure deserves intelligent, real-time monitoring — not periodic manual surveys and reactive maintenance.

Starting from Nagpur's MIDC industrial zone, we built proprietary AI models, drone platforms, and geospatial analytics systems from the ground up — purpose-built for the harsh realities of mines, railways, highways, and forests.

Today, our systems are deployed across 25+ cities, trusted by Coal India, Indian Railways, NHAI, and state forestry departments — monitoring over 500 million square feet of critical infrastructure annually.

What Drives Us
