Platform Capabilities

Everything Your Mine Needs in One System

From autonomous drone flights to AI-generated compliance reports — our platform handles the full monitoring lifecycle without manual intervention.

Autonomous Drone Operations

Pre-programmed flight missions execute automatically on schedule, covering the entire mine site without operator input.

Real-Time Safety Monitoring

AI detects PPE violations, restricted zone intrusions, and equipment proximity hazards with instant alerts to site managers.

Volumetric Analytics

Automated stockpile volume calculations from every flight, with trend analysis and reconciliation against production data.

Pit Progression Tracking

Compare current pit geometry against mine plan at any time, with automated deviation alerts and progress reports.

Automated Compliance Reports

Regulatory-ready reports generated automatically after each survey — reducing reporting time from days to minutes.

Unified Operations Dashboard

Single dashboard consolidating all drone data, AI alerts, volumetric trends, and compliance status for site managers.
