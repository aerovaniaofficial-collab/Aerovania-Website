Key Challenges

Protecting Forests at Scale

Deforestation & Encroachment

Illegal felling and land encroachment in forest areas are difficult to detect and enforce across vast, remote territories.

Forest Fire Risk

Early detection of fire-prone dry vegetation and active fire fronts is critical to preventing large-scale forest loss.

Biodiversity Monitoring

Tracking wildlife habitats, species distribution, and ecosystem health across large forest areas requires aerial intelligence.

Carbon Stock Assessment

Accurate biomass estimation and carbon stock measurement are essential for climate reporting and carbon credit programmes.

Our Solutions

Aerovania for Environmental & Forestry

Forest Cover Mapping

High-resolution aerial surveys classify forest types, canopy density, and land cover change using multi-spectral imagery and AI.

Fire Risk & Detection

Thermal and NDVI analysis identifies dry vegetation hotspots and active fire fronts for early warning and rapid response.

Biomass & Carbon Estimation

LiDAR-based canopy height models and AI algorithms estimate above-ground biomass and carbon stocks with high accuracy.

Encroachment Monitoring

Change detection algorithms identify illegal felling, agricultural encroachment, and mining activity within forest boundaries.

Wildlife Habitat Assessment

Map and monitor wildlife corridors, nesting sites, and habitat fragmentation to support conservation planning.

Compliance Reporting

Automated generation of forest survey reports, diversion proposals, and environmental impact assessments for regulatory submission.
