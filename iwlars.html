Platform

IWLARS

Intelligent Wide-Area Land and Resource Surveillance — a persistent, AI-powered monitoring platform for large-scale land, resource, and infrastructure surveillance across remote and critical areas.

Request a Demo Platform Features
Platform Overview

Wide-Area Intelligence, Continuously

IWLARS combines persistent aerial surveillance, AI analytics, and geospatial intelligence to give operators a continuous, real-time picture of large and remote areas.

Wide-Area Coverage

Persistent surveillance of areas spanning hundreds of square kilometres using coordinated drone fleets and fixed sensors.

Continuous Monitoring

24/7 automated surveillance with AI-powered change detection that flags anomalies without human review of every frame.

Geospatial Intelligence Layer

All surveillance data is geo-referenced and overlaid on a live GIS map, giving operators full spatial context for every alert.

Intelligent Alerting

AI classifies detected events by type and severity, routing alerts to the right teams with full context and location data.

Historical Analysis

Full archive of surveillance data enables retrospective analysis, trend identification, and evidence collection.

Multi-Sensor Fusion

Integrates data from drones, fixed cameras, satellite imagery, and IoT sensors into a single unified intelligence picture.

Applications

Where IWLARS Operates

Mining & Resource Areas

Monitor large mine leases, detect illegal mining, and track environmental compliance across vast areas.

Forest & Conservation Zones

Persistent surveillance of protected forest areas to detect encroachment, poaching, and illegal felling.

Border & Security Zones

Wide-area border surveillance with AI-powered intrusion detection and rapid response coordination.

Power & Pipeline Corridors

Continuous monitoring of transmission line and pipeline corridors for encroachments and anomalies.

Urban Periphery

Monitor city boundaries and peri-urban areas for unauthorised development and land use change.

Water Bodies & Catchments

Surveillance of reservoir catchments, river corridors, and coastal areas for encroachment and pollution.
