Industry Challenges

What Mining Operations Face

Safety Hazards

Slope failures, equipment proximity violations, and blast zone breaches put workers at constant risk.

Inaccurate Volumetrics

Manual stockpile surveys are slow, dangerous, and prone to significant measurement errors.

Compliance Delays

Regulatory reporting requires frequent site surveys that disrupt operations and consume resources.

Limited Visibility

Large mine footprints make it impossible to monitor all areas simultaneously with ground teams.
