About Aerovania

India's aerial intelligence company — building the systems that turn raw data from the sky into verified, actionable intelligence for the industries that shape the world.

Our Story Get in Touch
Who We Are

Turning Vision into Verified Reality

Aerovania is an aerial intelligence company headquartered in Nagpur, Maharashtra. We design, build, and deploy end-to-end drone intelligence systems for India's most demanding industries.

From open-cast mines to national highways, railway corridors to defence perimeters — our platforms combine purpose-built UAVs, multi-sensor payloads, and AI analytics into a single, field-proven solution.

  • Purpose-built drone platforms for industrial-grade data acquisition
  • AI-powered analytics delivering actionable intelligence, not just data
  • End-to-end ownership — hardware, software, and services under one roof
  • Headquartered in Nagpur, serving clients across India and beyond
Our Values

What Drives Us

Every decision we make — from hardware design to client delivery — is guided by these core principles.

Precision First

We engineer for accuracy at every layer — from sensor calibration to AI model validation. ±2cm is not a target, it's a baseline.

Field-Proven Reliability

Every system we deploy is tested in real operating conditions — not just labs. If it can't survive the field, it doesn't leave our facility.

Intelligence Over Data

We don't deliver raw data — we deliver verified intelligence. Our clients get answers, not files to interpret.

Client Partnership

We work alongside our clients from scoping to deployment. Long-term relationships, not one-off transactions.

Responsible Innovation

Technology must serve people and planet. Every system we build considers its environmental and social footprint.

Built to Scale

From a single survey to a national monitoring network — our architecture scales without compromise on quality or accuracy.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

From a small team of engineers with a shared conviction — that aerial intelligence could transform India's infrastructure industries — to a full-stack drone intelligence company.

01

Founded in Nagpur

Aerovania was founded with a clear mission: build aerial intelligence systems that actually work in India's demanding field conditions.

02

First Industrial Deployments

Early deployments in mining and highway surveying validated our approach — precision data, delivered faster and safer than traditional methods.

03

AI Platform Development

We built our own AI analytics stack — trained on domain-specific aerial datasets — to move beyond data collection into true intelligence delivery.

04

Multi-Industry Expansion

Today we serve defence, railways, power, oil & gas, smart cities, and environmental sectors — with a unified platform and dedicated industry teams.
