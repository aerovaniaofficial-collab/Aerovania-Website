Why Aerovania

Work on Problems That Actually Matter

At Aerovania, you're not building another app — you're building systems that protect borders, monitor forests, and keep infrastructure safe. The work is hard, the stakes are real, and the impact is visible.

We're a small, focused team where every person's contribution shapes the product. No bureaucracy, no silos — just engineers solving hard problems together.

  • Work on real hardware deployed in real field conditions
  • Own your work end-to-end — from design to deployment
  • Collaborate directly with founders and senior engineers
  • Competitive compensation and equity for key roles
Culture

What It's Like to Work Here

We hire for curiosity, rigour, and ownership. Here's what you can expect from day one.

Move Fast, Build Right

We move quickly but never cut corners on quality. Speed and precision are not opposites — we prove that every day.

Hands-On Engineering

Our engineers get their hands dirty — literally. Field deployments, hardware testing, and real-world validation are part of the job.

Continuous Learning

Drone tech, AI, and geospatial science move fast. We invest in our team's growth through training, conferences, and research time.

Real-World Impact

Your work will be deployed in mines, on highways, and at borders. The feedback loop from lab to field is short and direct.

Tight-Knit Team

Small enough that everyone knows everyone. Big enough to tackle complex, multi-disciplinary problems across hardware and software.

Based in Nagpur

Our HQ is in Nagpur, Maharashtra — with field operations across India. Remote and hybrid arrangements considered for the right roles.
