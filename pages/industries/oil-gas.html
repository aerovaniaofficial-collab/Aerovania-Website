Industry Challenges

Critical Risks in Oil & Gas

Pipeline Integrity

Thousands of kilometres of pipelines are vulnerable to corrosion, third-party damage, and encroachments that are hard to detect manually.

Leak Detection

Gas and liquid leaks pose explosion risks and environmental damage, yet traditional detection methods are slow and reactive.

Facility Inspection

Inspecting refineries, storage tanks, and offshore platforms safely and efficiently is a persistent operational challenge.

Environmental Compliance

Monitoring spill containment, flaring, and land disturbance around facilities requires continuous aerial surveillance.

Aerovania for Oil & Gas
