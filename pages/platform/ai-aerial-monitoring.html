Platform

AI Driven Aerial Monitoring & Analytics

A comprehensive analytics dashboard for monitoring mine operations — with real-time violation detection, geospatial site management, video intelligence, and role-based access control.

Request a Demo Platform Features
Analytics & Monitoring

Real-Time Dashboard & Map Intelligence

Get a complete operational picture with live KPIs, violation statistics, and time-series trend charts — all in one unified dashboard. Violations are instantly plotted on an interactive geospatial map with custom markers so you can pinpoint incidents across all mine sites in real time.

  • Live KPIs, charts, and violation statistics
  • Interactive map with geospatial violation markers
  • Searchable, filterable violation data table with pagination
  • Time-series analysis of violation trends over time
Site & Video Management

Manage Sites, Features & Surveillance Videos

Dynamically create and manage mining sites with built-in duplicate prevention. Pre-loaded with BNK Mines, Dhori, Kathara, and Bukaro. Link surveillance videos to specific sites and features — with YouTube thumbnail generation, search and filter, and role-based access.

  • Add, edit, and delete sites with validation
  • Video links organised by site and feature
  • YouTube thumbnail auto-generation
  • Admin-only creation, read-only for regular users
Security & Document Management

Role-Based Access & ATR Document Control

JWT-secured authentication with separate admin and user roles. ATR (Accident/Incident Report) PDFs are stored securely on Cloudinary, organised by department with colour-coded badges and drag-and-drop upload.

  • JWT authentication with role-based access control
  • Departments: E&T, Security, Operation, Survey, Safety
  • Drag-and-drop PDF upload to department folders
  • Admin cross-department access with colour-coded badges
How It Works

From Data Capture to Actionable Insight

1

Aerial Data Capture

Drones capture high-resolution imagery and video across the mine site, feeding data directly into the platform.

2

AI Violation Detection

AI models process footage in real time — detecting PPE non-compliance, restricted zone breaches, and safety hazards.

3

Dashboard & Map Sync

Violations are instantly plotted on the interactive map and reflected in KPI dashboards, tables, and trend charts.

4

Reports & Compliance

ATR documents, violation exports, and compliance summaries generated and stored — accessible by role and department.
