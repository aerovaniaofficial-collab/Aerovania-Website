Platform

Intelligent Wide-Area
Land & Resource Surveillance

IWLARS a persistent, AI-powered monitoring platform for large-scale land, resource, and infrastructure surveillance across remote and critical areas.

Request a Demo Platform Features
Wide-Area Coverage

Persistent Surveillance Across Hundreds of km

IWLARS deploys coordinated drone fleets and fixed sensor networks to maintain continuous coverage of areas spanning hundreds of square kilometres including remote, inaccessible, and critical zones.

  • Up to 500 km continuous coverage area
  • Coordinated multi-drone fleet operations
  • Fixed sensor integration for perimeter zones
  • 24/7 automated surveillance with no coverage gaps
Geospatial Intelligence

Live GIS Map with Full Spatial Context

Every surveillance event is geo-referenced and instantly plotted on a live GIS map. Operators get full spatial context for every alert with historical overlays, zone boundaries, and change detection layers all in one view.

  • Real-time geo-referenced event plotting
  • Custom zone boundaries and exclusion areas
  • Historical change detection overlays
  • Multi-layer GIS with satellite and drone imagery
AI Alerting & Sensor Fusion

Intelligent Alerts from Fused Multi-Sensor Data

IWLARS fuses data from drones, fixed cameras, satellite imagery, and IoT sensors into a single intelligence picture. AI classifies every detected event by type and severity, routing alerts to the right teams instantly.

  • AI event classification by type and severity
  • Under 60-second alert response time
  • Drone, camera, satellite & IoT data fusion
  • Role-based alert routing with full event context
How It Works

From Sensor to Actionable Intelligence

1

Deploy & Configure

Drone fleets and fixed sensors are deployed across the target area with defined coverage zones and alert boundaries.

2

Continuous Data Capture

Sensors stream live imagery, video, and telemetry 24/7 into the IWLARS platform for real-time processing.

3

AI Analysis & Detection

AI models analyse incoming data, detecting anomalies, intrusions, and changes against defined baselines.

4

Alert & Response

Classified alerts are routed to the right teams with full context, location, and evidence for rapid response.
