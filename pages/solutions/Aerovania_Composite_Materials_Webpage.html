About Our Technology

WHERE NATURE MEETS ENGINEERING

Pioneering Sustainable Composites

Aerovania's composite division bridges traditional materials science with modern sustainability requirements. Our proprietary Jute-Glass Hybrid Composite (JGHC) technology delivers near-carbon-fibre structural performance at significantly lower cost and environmental impact.

🌿
Natural Fibre Reinforcement
Farm-harvested jute fibres from West Bengal and Assam form the primary reinforcement — eliminating dependence on synthetic petrochemical fibres.
⚗️
Synthetic Resin Matrix
High-performance epoxy and polyester resin systems bind the natural fibre matrix, delivering exceptional tensile and flexural properties.
🏭
In-House Carbon Fibre Manufacturing
India's most compact indigenous carbon fibre production facility — enabling full vertical integration from raw material to finished composite component.

Research & Development

R&D EXPERTISE

Our materials science team combines academic rigour with real-world manufacturing experience. Current R&D focus areas span multi-scale modelling, hybrid material optimisation, sustainable binders, and rapid prototyping for OEM qualification.

01
Hybrid Laminate Stack Optimisation
Fibre angle, ply count and stacking sequence optimisation for targeted anisotropic stiffness in UAV frame, automotive panel and structural applications.
02
Natural Fibre Surface Treatment R&D
Alkali, silane and NaOH treatment studies to maximise fibre-matrix adhesion in jute, bamboo and hemp reinforced systems. Wet ageing characterisation.
03
Carbon Fibre Process Optimisation
Polyacrylonitrile (PAN) precursor stabilisation, oxidation and high-temperature carbonisation process parameter studies for consistent T300-grade output.
04
End-of-Life Biodegradation Studies
Accelerated soil burial and UV weathering tests to quantify long-term biodegradation pathways of JGHC panels. Supporting eco-certification requirements.
05
Multi-Industry Component Qualification
ASTM/ISO mechanical testing, RDSO compliance studies, automotive crash simulation, and aerospace airworthiness documentation support for OEM qualification.
