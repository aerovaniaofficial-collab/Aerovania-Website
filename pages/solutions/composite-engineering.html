Research & Development

R&D EXPERTISE

Our materials science team combines academic rigour with real-world manufacturing experience. Current R&D focus areas span multi-scale modelling, hybrid material optimisation, sustainable binders, and rapid prototyping for OEM qualification.

01
Hybrid Laminate Stack Optimisation
Fibre angle, ply count and stacking sequence optimisation for targeted anisotropic stiffness in UAV frame, automotive panel and structural applications.
02
Natural Fibre Surface Treatment R&D
Alkali, silane and NaOH treatment studies to maximise fibre-matrix adhesion in natural fibre reinforced systems. Wet ageing characterisation.
03
Carbon Fibre Process Optimisation
PAN precursor stabilisation, oxidation and high-temperature carbonisation process parameter studies for consistent T300-grade output.
04
End-of-Life Biodegradation Studies
Accelerated soil burial and UV weathering tests to quantify long-term biodegradation pathways of natural fibre composite panels. Supporting eco-certification requirements.
05
Multi-Industry Component Qualification
ASTM/ISO mechanical testing, RDSO compliance studies, automotive crash simulation, and aerospace airworthiness documentation support for OEM qualification.
