What We Offer

Geospatial Intelligence at Scale

From LiDAR point clouds to multi-spectral imagery, our platform delivers centimetre-level accuracy across vast terrain.

High-Resolution Mapping

Sub-centimetre orthomosaic maps and 3D terrain models from drone imagery using photogrammetry and AI-assisted processing.

LiDAR Point Cloud Processing

Classify, filter, and analyse dense LiDAR point clouds to extract ground models, vegetation layers, and structural features.

Change Detection & Analytics

Automatically detect terrain changes, encroachments, and volumetric shifts over time using multi-temporal comparison algorithms.

GIS Integration

Seamlessly export to ArcGIS, QGIS, and custom GIS platforms. Full support for GeoTIFF, SHP, and LAS formats.

Autonomous Flight Planning

AI-optimised flight path generation for maximum coverage efficiency, obstacle avoidance, and regulatory compliance.

Multi-Spectral Analysis

Leverage NDVI, thermal, and hyperspectral imaging to assess vegetation health, moisture levels, and surface anomalies.

Applications

Where Geospatial AI Delivers

Mining Volumetrics

Accurate stockpile volume calculations and pit progression monitoring without manual surveys.

Highway Corridor Mapping

End-to-end corridor surveys for alignment planning, ROW verification, and construction monitoring.

Railway Track Inspection

Detect track geometry deviations, ballast degradation, and encroachments along rail corridors.

Forest Canopy Analysis

Map forest cover, estimate biomass, and monitor deforestation using aerial LiDAR and imagery.

Power Line Corridor

Identify vegetation encroachment, sag analysis, and structural anomalies along transmission lines.

Urban Planning

3D city modelling, land use classification, and infrastructure planning for smart city development.
