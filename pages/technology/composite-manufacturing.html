Carbon Fibre Airframe Fabrication

Aerovania fabricates its own carbon fibre composite airframes in-house achieving the strength-to-weight ratios required for long-endurance, high-payload UAV operations. Every layup is hand-crafted and vacuum-bagged for consistent quality.

  • Unidirectional and woven carbon fibre laminates
  • Vacuum infusion and prepreg layup processes
  • Autoclave and oven-cured structural components
  • Non-destructive testing (NDT) on all structural parts

Structural Optimisation

Lightweight Structural Optimisation

Every gram matters in UAV design. Aerovania's structural engineers use topology optimisation and FEA simulation to remove material where it isn't needed maximising payload capacity and flight endurance without compromising structural integrity.

  • Topology optimisation for minimum weight structures
  • FEA-validated structural margins for all load cases
  • Sandwich panel construction for stiffness-to-weight
  • Fatigue life analysis for repeated field use

Quality & Testing

Quality Control & Structural Testing

Every composite component undergoes dimensional inspection, NDT, and structural load testing before it leaves the workshop. Aerovania's quality process ensures every airframe that reaches the field meets the same standard as the first.

  • Ultrasonic NDT for delamination and void detection
  • Static load testing to design limit loads
  • Dimensional inspection with CMM and laser scanning
  • Full traceability of materials and processes
