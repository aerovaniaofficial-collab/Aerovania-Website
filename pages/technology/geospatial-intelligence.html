High-Resolution Orthomosaic Mapping

Aerial photogrammetry produces geometrically corrected, centimetre-resolution orthomosaics of any site — from open-cast mines and highway corridors to urban areas and forest reserves. Every pixel is georeferenced and survey-accurate.

  • Sub-5cm ground sampling distance (GSD)
  • RTK GNSS georeferencing for survey-grade accuracy
  • GeoTIFF output compatible with all GIS platforms
  • Automated mosaicking and colour balancing

3D Modelling

3D Terrain & Elevation Models

LiDAR and photogrammetry combine to produce Digital Terrain Models (DTM), Digital Surface Models (DSM), and full 3D point clouds — enabling volumetric calculations, slope analysis, flood modelling, and engineering design at survey-grade accuracy.

  • DTM, DSM, and CHM generation from LiDAR
  • Volumetric calculations for stockpiles and excavations
  • Slope, aspect, and contour analysis
  • LAS, LAZ, and DXF point cloud outputs

GIS Integration & Spatial Analytics

All outputs are delivered in standard GIS formats — GeoTIFF, Shapefile, KML, GeoJSON, and DXF — ready for direct import into ArcGIS, QGIS, AutoCAD, and other platforms. Spatial analytics layers add intelligence on top of raw geometry.

  • Compatible with ArcGIS, QGIS, AutoCAD, and more
  • GeoTIFF, SHP, KML, GeoJSON, DXF, LAS outputs
  • Attribute-rich vector layers for asset management
  • Spatial query and analysis support
