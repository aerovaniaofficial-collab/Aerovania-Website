Industry Challenges

Railway's Toughest Problems

Track Geometry Defects

Detecting rail misalignment, gauge variations, and ballast degradation across thousands of kilometres of track.

Encroachments

Illegal structures and settlements along railway corridors create safety hazards and operational disruptions.

Vegetation Overgrowth

Unchecked vegetation growth near tracks causes signal interference, derailment risks, and visibility issues.

Bridge & Culvert Condition

Thousands of railway bridges require regular inspection but traditional methods are slow and resource-intensive.

Our Solutions

Aerovania for Railways

Track Inspection & Geometry

LiDAR and photogrammetry-based track surveys detect geometry deviations, rail wear, and ballast conditions at speed.

Encroachment Detection

AI-powered analysis of aerial imagery identifies and maps encroachments along railway land boundaries for enforcement action.

Vegetation Clearance Mapping

Automated identification of vegetation within safety zones, with prioritised clearance schedules and progress tracking.

Bridge & Structure Inspection

Drone-based visual inspection of railway bridges, culverts, and retaining walls with AI-assisted defect classification.

Corridor Asset Mapping

Complete inventory of railway corridor assets — signals, OHE structures, level crossings, and stations — in a GIS database.

Disaster Response Surveys

Rapid post-flood, landslide, or derailment aerial surveys to assess damage and plan restoration operations.
